About Jordan

After graduating from Cleveland State University with a degree in Political Science, I went on to attend the Case Western Reserve University Full Stack Web Development program. My academic career has solidified my interest in creative problem solving and was a driving factor in my interest in coding and web development.

I am passionate about creating web pages that are just as aesthetically pleasing as they are functional. I have utilized HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery and Bootstrap to create a variety of applications. I also have experience with Node.js and AJAX. Based in Cleveland, Ohio, I am available to discuss your web development needs via video conference, phone call or email.
